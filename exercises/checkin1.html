Butter Cookies Recipe

This recipe will guide you through making delicious homemade butter cookies that can be used for any occasion.

What do you need?

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/3 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • sprinkles, for coating (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Beat the butter until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add the sugar and continue beating until the mixture is light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time, beating between each addition, then add the vanilla.
  3. Add the flour and then beat just until combined.
  4. Divide the dough in half then shape each piece of dough into a log. Wrap each dough log securely in plastic wrap and refrigerate them for a minimum of 1 hour.
  5. Remove the dough from the fridge, then roll each log in sprinkles (optional).
  6. Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  7. Working with one log at a time, slice the log into 1/4-inch rounds. Arrange the cookies on the baking sheets, spacing them 1 inch apart. Freeze the cookies for 10 minutes.
  8. Bake the cookies for 12 to 15 minutes until set. Once done baking, place them on cooling rack.

Notes & Tips:

Here are some things to consider when you are baking these cookies...

  • Do not let the cookies get golden brown when baking!
  • The cookie dough can be frozen up to 2 months.
  • After your cookies are baked, make sure to store them in airtight container.
  • When mixing your ingredients, avoid over mixing. This can cause your cookies to be dense and not taste as good.
